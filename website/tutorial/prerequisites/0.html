This section of the tutorial lists everything you need to start working with DESMO-J. Before you delve into modelling guided by the sections Processes Step by Step or Events Step by Step you should take a look at what is required in terms of tools and knowledge.

Tools

The sub-section Tools lists all the necessary tools and sources you need to successfully build a DESMO-J-based simulation.

Knowledge

The sub-section Knowledge covers the background information you need in advance if you want to successfully follow the tutorial to the goal of building a DESMO-J-based simulation model.
